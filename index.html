Questions and answers

How long does it take to develop a website?

The time frame depends on the complexity of the project. An AURA-style landing page usually takes 7 to 14 days, while complex web applications can take a month or more to develop.

Do you work with ready-made layouts?

Yes, we can implement your idea using an existing design in Figma or develop a concept from scratch based on your preferences and business objectives.

What technologies do you use?

We use a modern stack: HTML5, CSS3 (SCSS), JavaScript (ES6+), React for the frontend and PHP/MySQL for the backend. We also work with WordPress.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Of course. Each client receives 30 days of free technical support and consultations after the official launch of the project.
